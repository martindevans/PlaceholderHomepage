Influence the direction the game takes in it's very earliest stages of development!

Explore A City

Explore hundreds of square kilometers of procedurally generated city: walk down every street, go into every building, enter every room and talk to every NPC.

Rob A Bank

Find a bank somewhere in the city, scout out the area, form a plan of attack and then infiltrate the bank, dodge guards, disable security systems and steal everything - just remember that the best bank Heist is the one that no one even realises has happened until the next day.

With Your Friends

Pulling off a bank heist on your own is really hard trust me, I tried. Play in the same city as your friends, even when you're offline, and when you've all scouted out the bank and got the necessary equipment go in and pull off the job together (you can't do this bit offline).

Mod The Game

Everything is a mod so you can customise your game as much as you like. Go to plugins.placeholder-software.co.uk and you can install new menus, buildings, city layouts, shaders, textures, models, guns, characters... I really did mean everything.

Create New Buildings

Hand design new buildings, rooms, floors or entire city blocks and then find them used in generated cities as you play. Coming Soon.

Share Your Creations

Upload your own creations to plugins.placeholder-software.co.uk and share your creations with the world. Everything from that building you designed in the building designer to a game project you spent months programming.
