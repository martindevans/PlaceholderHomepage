A Life Of Crime

Sneak into the building disguised as an employee, fast talk your way past guards, crack the safe code, steal the corporate secrets and disappear before anyone even knows a crime has been committed.

Smash down the doors to the bank, shoot the guards, take everyone in the lobby hostage, blow open the safe with high explosives, take the bullion and flee the scene before the police arrive.

Where you target, what you steal and how you commit your crimes is up to you. The only rule is not to get caught!

Entire Worlds

Sometimes the best way to break into a bank is to jump onto the roof from the next building over. Sometimes the best way to carry out an assassination is to snipe the target and all his guards from across the street.

Heist generates entire city blocks for you to perform your crimes in. Find the shady back alleys to sneak in, the badly secured neighbouring building to bypass security and the perfect sniping spot to take out the guards.

Modding

The entire game has been built from the ground up for modding. New buildings, characters, NPC behaviours, weapons and even entire gamemodes can be added by plugins.

This means that updates to the game can come quickly - optional new parts can always be made available to everyone, by anyone. You can install the plugins you want to customise the game to how you and your friends play.

Early Access

Heist is currently in development, this means that most of the planned features of the game don't exist yet and large design changes could still happen. Nothing is finalised, major features are still in development and game breaking bugs are still common.

Those large design changes will happen in response to what feedback I get from players, from you. By getting involved early in development you can help shape how the game develops.
